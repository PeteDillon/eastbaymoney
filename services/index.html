What I don't do
Manage your investments
I don't take custody of your assets, execute trades on your behalf, or charge a percentage of assets under management. I will advise you on your investment strategy, but the implementation stays in your hands. If you want a great investment manager, I'm happy to recommend one.
Sell financial products
No insurance policies, annuities, mutual funds, or any other financial product. I receive no commissions and have no financial relationship with any product provider. My only financial interest is in giving you good advice.
Prepare your tax returns
My focus is tax planning — the strategy that happens before the return. As a licensed CPA, I can discuss the full picture, but I don't offer standalone tax preparation or filing services. If that's what you primarily need, I can point you toward someone great.
Give legal advice
I'm not an attorney. I can review documents, flag issues I see, and help you think through legal and structural decisions — but producing wills, trusts, operating agreements, or other legal documents is your attorney's job, not mine.
